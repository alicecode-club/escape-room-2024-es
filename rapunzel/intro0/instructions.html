Instructions

Oh no! Seems like Mother Gothel will be returning soon, but she forgot the keys around the house. This might be Rapunzel's only aportunity to escape. Will you help her?

How to play:

Rapunzel is stuck in the kitchen at the moment, but there's a key hidden somewhere in the room. Help her find it by finding the missing object, correctly answering a question and finding a secret code and a chest that will open it!

The door you'll open will lead you to the next room. There are 4 rooms in total. You will have to complete the same tasks there but with different questions, objects and codes!
